Crypto Trading Lessons

Learn everything about crypto trading, swapping, buying, and selling

Introduction to Crypto

Cryptocurrencies are digital assets built on blockchain technology. They allow for secure, decentralized transactions. Blockchain is a distributed ledger that records all transactions across a network of computers.

How to Trade Crypto

Spot trading involves buying and selling cryptocurrencies at market prices. Strategies include day trading, swing trading, and long-term investing. Risk management is key to success.

Swapping Tokens

Use DEXs like Uniswap and PancakeSwap to instantly swap tokens. Learn about slippage, gas fees, liquidity pools, and how automated market makers (AMMs) work.

Buying Crypto

Choose exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken. Understand KYC, payment methods (bank, card, P2P), and secure wallet storage options.

Selling Crypto

Convert crypto to fiat via exchanges, P2P, or crypto cards. Consider withdrawal limits, verification steps, and local tax laws.

Using Crypto Wallets

Hot wallets (MetaMask, Trust Wallet) vs cold wallets (Ledger, Trezor). Learn how to protect your private keys and recover seed phrases.

Understanding Market Trends

Track bullish and bearish markets using technical analysis: support/resistance levels, RSI, MACD, and candlestick patterns.

DeFi Basics

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) allows you to earn, lend, and borrow crypto without intermediaries. Explore yield farming, staking, and liquidity mining.

Security Tips

Use 2FA, avoid phishing scams, and never share your seed phrase. Keep software up-to-date and use trusted platforms only.

ICO & IDO Participation

Initial Coin Offerings (ICO) and Initial DEX Offerings (IDO) offer early access to tokens. Research projects carefully to avoid rug pulls.

Stablecoins

Stablecoins like USDT and USDC are pegged to fiat. Learn how they provide a safe haven in volatile markets.
